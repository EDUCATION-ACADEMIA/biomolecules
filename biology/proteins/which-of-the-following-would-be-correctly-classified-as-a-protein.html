Which of the following would be correctly classified as a protein?

(A) Cholestrol

(B) ** Enzyme

(C) Starch

(D) Cellulose

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is (d) Amino acids. A protein is defined as a polymeric molecule consisting of a chain of amino acids. In living organisms, proteins have essential functions, like providing structural support and acting as enzymes to catalyze chemical reactions.

Concept note-2: -They speed up biochemical reactions. Enzymes are proteins that catalyze biochemical reactions. A catalyst is a chemical that speeds up chemical reactions. Enzymes bring reactants together forming an enzyme-substrate complex.

Concept note-3: -Final answer: DNA is not a protein.
