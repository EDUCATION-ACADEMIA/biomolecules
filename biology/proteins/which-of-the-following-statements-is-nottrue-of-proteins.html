Which of the following statements is NOTtrue of proteins?

(A) Proteins are the most functionally diverse group of molecules in living systems

(B) ** Special storage proteins guarantee an adequate amount of amino acids is present for protein synthesis

(C) Immunoglobulins involved in fighting pathogens are proteins

(D) All proteins are linear polymers of amino acids

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is A. The sites of protein synthesis are the mitochondria of cells.. This is not a true statement about protein synthesis.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer option is (B). The process of synthesis of proteins according to the sequence of messenger ribonucleic acid (mRNA) is known as translation. The process of translation is catalyzed by ribosome molecules.

Concept note-3: -Thus, the correct answer is option ‘They contain genetic information’.

Concept note-4: -Enzymes are proteins made up of amino acids and acts as biological catalysts in our body. However, glucose serves the purpose of energy provider for metabolism which is not a protein.
