silk fibroin consist of polypeptide chain that are arranged in the form of

(A) alpha helix

(B) beta helix

(C) ** beta pleated sheet

(D) none of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Silk fibroin is composed of -sheet crystallites (hereafter simply referred to as crystallites) and amorphous domains [2, 3].

Concept note-2: -The secondary structure of silk is the beta pleated sheet. The primary structure of silk contains the amino acids of glycine, alanine, serine, in specific repeating pattern. These three amino acids make up 90% of the protein in silk. The last 10% is comprised of the amino acids glutamic acid, valine, and aspartic acid.

Concept note-3: -The heavy fibroin protein consists of layers of antiparallel beta sheets. Its primary structure mainly consists of the recurrent amino acid sequence (Gly-Ser-Gly-Ala-Gly-Ala)n.

Concept note-4: -It consists of several monomers arranged together in a three-dimensional space. Thus, based on the above information we can conclude that a β -pleated sheet organization in a polypeptide chain is an example of 2 ∘ structure. Hence, the correct answer is option (B).
