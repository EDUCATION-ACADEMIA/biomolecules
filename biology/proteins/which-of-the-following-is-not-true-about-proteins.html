Which of the following is not true about proteins?

(A) ** Proteins are essential for the transport and storage of fat-soluble vitamins in the body

(B) Enzymes are proteins which act as biological catalysts.

(C) Proteins are involved in the maintenance and regulation of life processes.

(D) Proteins are the major structural materials of animal tissue.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is A. The sites of protein synthesis are the mitochondria of cells.. This is not a true statement about protein synthesis.

Concept note-2: -However, proteins do not provide energy for metabolism.

Concept note-4: -Final answer: DNA is not a protein.
