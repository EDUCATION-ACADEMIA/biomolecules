If a third amino acid combines to a ____, the product is called a ____

(A) ** dipeptide, tripeptide

(B) dipeptide, dipeptide

(C) tripeptide, tripeptide

(D) tripeptide, dipeptide

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The end product obtained is called dipeptide as it is made up of two amino acids. Similarly, when third amino acids combine to dipeptide then the product obtained is called tripeptide.

Concept note-2: -Let 3 amino acids be ABC. The acid and amine ends of each molecule can be used to produce a tripeptide. Hence a total of 9 tripeptides are possible.

Concept note-3: -The simplest tripeptide is glycine. In terms of scientific investigations, the dominant tripeptide is glutathione (-L-Glutamyl-L-cysteinylglycine), which serves many roles in many forms of life.

Concept note-4: -A dipeptide has two amino acids. A tripeptide has three amino acids. A tetrapeptide has four amino acids.

Concept note-5: -A chain consisting of only two amino acid units is called a dipeptide; a chain consisting of three is a tripeptide.
