Catalysts perform which of the following functions?

(A) Carry essential nutrients around our bodies

(B) ** Assist in chemical reactions

(C) Convert DNA into RNA

(D) Provide structure for our bodies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A catalyst is a substance that speeds up a chemical reaction, or lowers the temperature or pressure needed to start one, without itself being consumed during the reaction.

Concept note-2: -Catalyst is a substance that increases the rate of reaction and the process is known as catalysis.

Concept note-3: -A catalyst is a substance that either increases or decreases the rate of a chemical reaction without itself undergoing any chemical change. here iron act as a catalyst and increases the rate of chemical reaction.
