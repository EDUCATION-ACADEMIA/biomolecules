Which of the following does not describe the link which forms between two amino acids?

(A) peptide link

(B) ** ester link

(C) amide link

(D) -CONH-

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A peptide bond is a covalent chemical bond linking two consecutive amino acid monomers along a peptide or protein chain.

Concept note-2: -The amino acids in the ribosome are attached to their respective tRNAs by an ester bond (R-O-R) between the carboxyl terminus and the amino acceptor stem (left).

Concept note-3: -A protein molecule is made from a long chain of these amino acids, each linked to its neighbor through a covalent peptide bond (Figure 3-1).
