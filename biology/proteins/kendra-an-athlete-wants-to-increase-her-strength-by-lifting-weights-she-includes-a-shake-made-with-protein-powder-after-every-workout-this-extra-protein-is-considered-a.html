Kendra, an athlete, wants to increase her strength by lifting weights. She includes a shake made with protein powder after every workout. This extra protein is considered a:

(A) Mistake

(B) Mineral

(C) ** Supplement

(D) Antioxidant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As a result, athletes may use protein powder to speed up recovery from muscle soreness after exercise. Many studies report that taking protein supplements after exercise can aid recovery by reducing muscle damage and improving muscle performance and muscle protein synthesis.

Concept note-2: -Whey-Based Protein Powders Decades of research shows that whey protein is one of the best building blocks to build muscle mass. This is because whey protein is rich in leucine, an amino acid that is responsible for stimulating muscle protein synthesis post-exercise.

Concept note-3: -There is growing research supporting the notion that protein taken in the morning gives a feeling of fullness and reduces appetite snacking. This can help with weight loss. Another positive factor to having a protein powder in the morning is that it can support strength and muscle mass.

Concept note-4: -Whey Protein Supplement promotes muscle growth and improves resistance training performance. Casein protein promotes fat loss and aids muscle growth. Egg-based supplements provide high-quality protein and are easy to digest.
