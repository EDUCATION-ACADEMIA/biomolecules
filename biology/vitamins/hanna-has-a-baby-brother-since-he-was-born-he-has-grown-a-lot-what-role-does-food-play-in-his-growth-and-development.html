Hanna has a baby brother. Since he was born, he has grown a lot. What role does food play in his growth and development?

(A) Food does not have a role in his growth and development.

(B) He likes milk and mashed bananas.

(C) ** Food provides vitamins, minerals, and energy.

(D) None of the above choices

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Carbohydrates and fats mainly provide energy to our body. Proteins and minerals are needed for the growth and the maintenance of our body.

Concept note-2: -The major nutrients in our food are carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins and minerals. In addition, food also contains dietary fibres and water. Carbohydrates and fats mainly provide energy to our body.

Concept note-3: -There are six basic nutrients: carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, and water. All of these are classified as essential. Your body requires essential nutrients to function properly. These nutrients must be obtained from the foods you eat; your body cannot make them on its own.
