Which nutrient carries nutrients and oxygen throughout the body?

(A) Dairy

(B) Minerals

(C) Protein

(D) ** Water

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Blood brings oxygen and nutrients to all the parts of the body so they can keep working. Blood carries carbon dioxide and other waste materials to the lungs, kidneys, and digestive system to be removed from the body.

Concept note-2: -Water carries nutrients and oxygen to all cells in the body. Water is the main property of blood, which carries nutrients to cells and carries wastes out of the body. Water helps convert food into energy. Water protects and cushions vital organs.

Concept note-3: -The circulatory system carries oxygen, nutrients, and hormones to cells, and removes waste products, like carbon dioxide. These roadways travel in one direction only, to keep things going where they should.

Concept note-4: -Water transports valuable nutrients to your body Water allows these nutrients to pass through the capillaries within the intestinal walls to the blood and circulatory system where the valuable nutrients and oxygen can be distributed throughout the body to all the cells and organs.
