Vitamins are needed only in ____ quantities

(A) Large

(B) ** Small

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vitamins and minerals are a form of nutrient (called micronutrients) that are needed in small amounts. Although micronutrients donâ€™t give us energy, they are involved in the metabolic processes that enable us to get energy from carbohydrates, protein and fat, which are also known as macronutrients.

Concept note-2: -Taking higher than recommended doses of some vitamins may cause health problems. Such as: Vitamins A, D, E and K are fat soluble, which means they are stored in the body and if taken in high doses can be toxic. High doses of some water-soluble vitamins, such as vitamin B6, can also become toxic.

Concept note-3: -Vitamins and minerals are often called micronutrients because your body needs only tiny amounts of them.

Concept note-4: -The given statement is true. Vitamins are the micronutrients which are consumed in very less amount. It assists in the treatment of deficiency diseases. Vitamins are considered micronutrients and not energy nutrients.
