What vitamin is found in foods like oranges, strawberries and broccoli?

(A) ** Vit C

(B) Vit A

(C) Vit K

(D) Vit E

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Broccoli contains 65 mg of vitamin C per 100 grams. One-half cup of steamed broccoli provides 57% of the DV for vitamin C and may lower your risk of inflammatory diseases.

Concept note-2: -Citrus fruits, tomatoes and tomato juice, and potatoes are major contributors of vitamin C to the American diet [8]. Other good food sources include red and green peppers, kiwifruit, broccoli, strawberries, Brussels sprouts, and cantaloupe (see Table 2) [8, 12].

Concept note-3: -Good sources of vitamin C citrus fruit, such as oranges and orange juice. peppers. strawberries. blackcurrants.

Concept note-4: -Broccoli. When people think of vitamin C, the first thing that (probably) comes to mind is citrus fruit. So, it’s surprising to hear that some veggies have *even* more vitamin C than an orange-but it’s true! One cup of chopped broccoli has 81 mg of vitamin C.
