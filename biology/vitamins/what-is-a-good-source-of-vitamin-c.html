What is a good source of vitamin C?

(A) ** Apple

(B) Lemon

(C) Orange

(D) Pineapple.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apples are a good source of fiber and vitamin C. They also contain antioxidants, like vitamin E, and polyphenols that contribute to the fruitâ€™s numerous health benefits.

Concept note-2: -Kakadu plums contain up to 2, 907 mg of vitamin C per 100 grams, making it the richest known source of this vitamin. Just one plum delivers around 484% of the DV.

Concept note-3: -The antioxidant power of flavonoids is one reason that apples are again in the spotlight. Apples contain only modest amounts of vitamin C. A medium apple averages about 6 milligrams of vitamin C, not much compared to the recommended daily intake of 75 to 90 milligrams for adults.

Concept note-4: -Benefits. Eat the apple peel, as it contains a significant portion of vitamin C within the apple. Vitamin C is a water-soluble vitamin.
