BIOLOGY

VITAMINS

[SOURCES]
Different vitamins are needed in different quantities.

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The given statement is true. Vitamins are the micronutrients which are consumed in very less amount. It assists in the treatment of deficiency diseases. Vitamins are considered micronutrients and not energy nutrients.

Concept note-2: -No one is metabolically identical or even similar to anyone else – not even identical twins. We all require different amounts of specific nutrients and vitamins.

Concept note-3: -Vitamins and minerals are a form of nutrient (called micronutrients) that are needed in small amounts. Although micronutrients don’t give us energy, they are involved in the metabolic processes that enable us to get energy from carbohydrates, protein and fat, which are also known as macronutrients.

Concept note-4: -Solution. False. Correct Statement: Vitamins are required in small quantities for normal functioning of the body.

Concept note-5: -Vitamins and minerals are micronutrients required by the body to carry out a range of normal functions. However, these micronutrients are not produced in our bodies and must be derived from the food we eat.
