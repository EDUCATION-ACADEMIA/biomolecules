VITAMINS

[SOURCES]
A vitamin is..

(A) All of these answers are correct:)

(B) A non-organic chemical naturally found in tablets

(C) ** An organic chemical naturally occurring in food

(D) An organic chemical unnaturally occurring in food

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vitamins are organic substances produced by plants or animals. They often are called “essential” because they are not synthesized in the body (except for vitamin D) and therefore must come from food.

Concept note-2: -A vitamin is an organic compound and a vital nutrient that an organism requires in limited amounts.

Concept note-3: -Vitamins are organic substances, which means they’re made by plants or animals. Minerals are inorganic elements that come from soil and water, and are absorbed by plants or eaten by animals. Your body needs larger amounts of some minerals, such as calcium, to grow and stay healthy.

Concept note-4: -As you may expect, natural or organic vitamins are made from plant material that naturally contains the vitamin being supplemented. For example, vitamin E is derived from vegetable oils, and vitamin C is found in oranges, along with several other plants.
