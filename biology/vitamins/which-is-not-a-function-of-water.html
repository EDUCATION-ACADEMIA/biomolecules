Which is NOT a function of water?

(A) Regulates body temperature

(B) ** Protects cell membranes

(C) Carries water-soluble vitamins

(D) Carries waste

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Therefore the correct answer is D) controlling movement into the nucleus since the cell membrane is not responsible for this function.

Concept note-2: -A) It provides energy for chemical reaction – Water does not provide energy for chemical reactions so it is not the function of water. It cannot provide energy so it is the correct answer.

Concept note-3: -Water molecules play an important role in providing unique environments for biological reactions on cell membranes. It is widely believed that water molecules form bridges that connect lipid molecules and stabilize cell membranes.

Concept note-4: -The four main functions of the plasma membrane include identification, communication, regulation of solute exchange through the membrane, and isolation of the cytoplasm from the external environment.
