Where can you find folic acid

(A) cod, salmon, tuna

(B) Beef, pork, lamb

(C) oranges, apples, pears

(D) ** Broccoli, sprouts, spinach

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Food Sources [38, 39] Good sources of folate include: Dark green leafy vegetables (turnip greens, spinach, romaine lettuce, asparagus, Brussels sprouts, broccoli)

Concept note-2: -leafy green vegetables, such as cabbage, kale, spring greens and spinach. peas. chickpeas and kidney beans. liver (but avoid this during pregnancy)

Concept note-3: -Dark leafy greens These have the highest folate levels of any food. Consider adding more spinach, kale, collard greens, turnip greens, romaine lettuce, raw cabbage, Brussel sprouts and broccoli to your diet for an immediate folic acid boost.
