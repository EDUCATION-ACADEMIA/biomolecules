VITAMINS

[SOURCES]
Vitamins are:

(A) ** MicroNutrients

(B) MacroNutrients

(C) Nutrients

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Micronutrients, often referred to as vitamins and minerals, are vital to healthy development, disease prevention, and wellbeing.

Concept note-2: -To break this down even further, macronutrients make up your total caloric intake, and include carbohydrates, proteins and fats. Micronutrients include vitamins, minerals and water.

Concept note-3: -Micronutrients can be divided into four groups-water-soluble vitamins, fat-soluble vitamins, macrominerals and trace minerals.

Concept note-4: -Vitamins and minerals are often called micronutrients because your body needs only tiny amounts of them. Yet failing to get even those small quantities virtually guarantees disease.
