Which food group is an orange in?

(A) Food from Animals

(B) Staples

(C) ** Fruit and Vegetables

(D) Fats and Oils

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What foods are in the Fruit Group? The Fruit Group includes all fruits and 100% fruit juice. Fruits may be fresh, frozen, canned, or dried/dehydrated.

Concept note-2: -Red and orange vegetables are high in beta-carotene, which the body turns into Vitamin A. Vitamin A helps protect against infection and some types of cancer. Many of these vegetables also have high levels of vitamin C, potassium, and vitamin K.

Concept note-3: -Some common deep orange vegetables include: carrots; butternut and acorn squash; sweet potatoes and yams, and pumpkins.
