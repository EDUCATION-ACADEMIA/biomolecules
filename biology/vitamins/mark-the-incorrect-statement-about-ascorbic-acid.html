Mark the INCORRECT statement about Ascorbic acid

(A) It is a strong reducing agent

(B) ** It can have synthesized in the body

(C) Involves in hydroxylation of prolyl-and lysyl-residues of collagen

(D) Shows antioxidant activity

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -7. Mark the INCORRECT statement about Ascorbic acid. Explanation: It can have synthesized in the body is incorrect as vertebrates such as birds, fishes, and humans are not able to synthesize vitamin C, due to lack of enzyme gulono-lactone oxidase.

Concept note-2: -15. Which of the following statements is false about Ascorbic acid? Sol: (c) It can be synthesized in the body. 16.

Concept note-3: -Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid), the antiscorbutic vitamin, cannot be synthesized by humans and other primates, and has to be obtained from diet.

Concept note-4: -15. Which of the following statements is false about Ascorbic acid? Answer: (c) It can be synthesized in the body.

Concept note-5: -In all cases so far studied, the inability to synthesize vitamin C is due to mutations in the L-gulono--lactone oxidase (GLO) gene which codes for the enzyme responsible for catalyzing the last step of vitamin C biosynthesis.
