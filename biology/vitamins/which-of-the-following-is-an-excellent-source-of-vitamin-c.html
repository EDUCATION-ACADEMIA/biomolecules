Which of the following is an excellent source of vitamin C?

(A) ** orange juice

(B) milk

(C) bread

(D) hamburger

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fruits and vegetables supply about 90 percent of the vitamin C in the diets of the general population. Oranges and 100% orange juice are one of the richest dietary sources of vitamin C8 and citrus juices are one of the most cost-effective ways to get vitamin C in the diet.

Concept note-2: -Citrus fruits, tomatoes and tomato juice, and potatoes are major contributors of vitamin C to the American diet [8]. Other good food sources include red and green peppers, kiwifruit, broccoli, strawberries, Brussels sprouts, and cantaloupe (see Table 2) [8, 12].

Concept note-3: -Citrus Juices Citrus fruits are near the top of the list for vitamin C-rich foods. Eight ounces of fresh-squeezed orange juice has nearly 125 milligrams of vitamin C. Diluted frozen orange juice from concentrate has a little lessâ€“about 95 milligrams per 8-ounce glass.
