what are 3 food sources of Vitamin B1

(A) apples, eggs, nuts

(B) ** eggs, liver, nuts

(C) liver, citrus fruit, red meat

(D) red meat, apples, citrus fruit

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In meat, liver has the highest amount of thiamine. Whereas three ounces of beef steak gives you 8% of your daily value of thiamine, one serving of beef liver will give you about 14%.

Concept note-2: -The yolks of fresh eggs contained on average 2.78 mg/kg vitamin B1 and the albumen 0.27 mg/kg.
