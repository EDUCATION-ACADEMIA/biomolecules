Which if the following foods are rich in Vitamin A?

(A) milk fortified with vitamin A

(B) liver

(C) orange fruits and vegetables (like cantaloupe, carrots, sweet potatoes)

(D) ** all of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You can also get vitamin A by including good sources of beta-carotene in your diet, as the body can convert this into retinol. The main food sources of beta-carotene are: yellow, red and green (leafy) vegetables, such as spinach, carrots, sweet potatoes and red peppers. yellow fruit, such as mango, papaya and apricots.

Concept note-2: -Mango is the fruit that has the highest Vitamin A.

Concept note-3: -Green vegetables like methi leaves, lettuce, spinach, and kale are excellent sources of vitamin A. They are also great sources of several nutrients like potassium, calcium, protein, and manganese.

Concept note-4: -Food rich in vitamin a are Carrots, Spinach, Sweet potato, Lettuce, etc.
