Concept note-1: -What Do Vitamins and Minerals Do? Vitamins and minerals boost the immune system, support normal growth and development, and help cells and organs do their jobs. For example, you’ve probably heard that carrots are good for your eyes. It’s true!

Concept note-2: -Vitamins and minerals boost our immunity, help in normal growth and development, and help cells and organs in their function. E.g. Carrots contain carotenoids that our body converts into vitamin A which is required by the eyes. Vitamin K helps in blood clotting.
