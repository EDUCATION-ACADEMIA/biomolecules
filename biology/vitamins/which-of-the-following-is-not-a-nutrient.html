Which of the following is not a nutrient?

(A) Water

(B) ** Sugar

(C) Carbohydrates

(D) Vitamins

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If you’re like many people, you’re probably eating and drinking more sugar than you realize. That’s because it’s added to so many foods and beverages. Added sugar contributes calories but not nutrients.

Concept note-2: -Sugars are carbohydrates. Like all carbohydrates, they provide a source of energy in our diet. Sugar is a term that includes all sweet carbohydrates, although the term is most often used to describe sucrose or table sugar, a ‘double sugar’.

Concept note-3: -8. Which of the following food components does not provide any nutrients? Sol: (b) Water.
