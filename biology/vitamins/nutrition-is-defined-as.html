NUTRITION is defined as:

(A) the study of nutrients

(B) living a healthy life

(C) ** The science or study of food and the ways in which the body uses the food.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nutrition is the study of nutrients in food, how the body uses them, and the relationship between diet, health, and disease.

Concept note-2: -Nutrition is the science of food, the nutrients, and other substances therein, their action, interaction, and balance in relation to health and disease and the processes by which the organism ingests, digests, absorbs, transports, utilizes, and excretes food substances.

Concept note-3: -Nutrition is the study of food and how it affects the health and growth of the body. Nutrients are substances found in foods that our bodies use to grow, reproduce and survive. Proteins are important for growth and development. They also help your body repair tissue, carry oxygen, digest foods, and regulate hormones.

Concept note-4: -Nutrition science also includes behaviors and social factors related to food choices. The foods we eat provide energy (calories) and nutrients such as protein, fat, carbohydrate, vitamins, minerals, and water.
