People who have a problem caused by too much or too little of a nutrient in their diets are said to suffer from ____

(A) Starvation

(B) ** Malnutrition

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Malnutrition refers to getting too little or too much of certain nutrients. It can lead to serious health issues, including stunted growth, eye problems, diabetes and heart disease. Malnutrition affects billions of people worldwide.

Concept note-2: -Malnutrition is an imbalance between the nutrients your body needs to function and the nutrients it gets. It can mean undernutrition or overnutrition.

Concept note-3: -Overnutrition is a form of malnutrition (imbalanced nutrition) arising from excessive intake of nutrients, leading to accumulation of body fat that impairs health (i.e., overweight/obesity)1.
