Name the vitamin which are essential for the health of the brain?

(A) vitamin B6

(B) vitamin B9

(C) vitamin B12

(D) ** all the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vitamin B6 is also called pyridoxine. Vitamin B6 helps form red blood cells and maintain brain function. This vitamin also plays an important role in the proteins that are part of many chemical reactions in the body.

Concept note-2: -B vitamins like B6, B12, and B9 (folic acid) all play a role in brain health.

Concept note-3: -The B vitamins – 1, 2, 3, 6, 9, and 12 – play an important role in brain health. They may help prevent dementia and boost the production of neurotransmitters – chemicals that deliver messages between neurons in the brain and body.

Concept note-4: -Abstract. Retinoid acid, the bioactive metabolite of vitamin A, is a potent signaling molecule in the brains of growing and adult animals, regulates numerous gene products, and modulates neurogenesis, neuronal survival and synaptic plasticity.
