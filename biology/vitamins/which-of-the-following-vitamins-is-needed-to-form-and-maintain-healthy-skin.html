Which of the following vitamins is needed to form and maintain healthy skin?

(A) Vitamin D

(B) ** Vitamin A

(C) Vitamin K

(D) Vitamin E

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vitamin A helps to speed up healing, prevent breakouts and support the skin’s immune system and it promotes natural moisturising-which means it helps to hydrate the skin effectively, giving it a radiant glow. It assists in promoting and maintaining a healthy dermis and epidermis; the top two layers of your skin.

Concept note-2: -Vitamin D is one of the best vitamins for your skin, along with vitamins C, E, and K. Making sure you get enough vitamins can keep your skin looking healthy and youthful.

Concept note-3: -Vitamin A, also known as retinol, has several important functions. These include: helping your body’s natural defence against illness and infection (the immune system) work properly. helping vision in dim light.
