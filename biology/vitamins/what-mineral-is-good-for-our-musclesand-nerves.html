What mineral is good for our musclesand nerves?

(A) Calcium

(B) Sodim

(C) Iron

(D) ** Potassium

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Potassium is needed for muscle contraction, communication between muscles and nerves, and overall muscular function. Since muscles are found throughout your body, including your arms, legs and respiratory and digestive tracts, a diet low in potassium can contribute to fatigue and digestive troubles.

Concept note-2: -Potassium is a mineral that your body needs to work properly. It is a type of electrolyte. It helps your nerves to function and muscles to contract.

Concept note-3: -Potassium is a mineral that your cells, nerves, and muscles need to function properly.

Concept note-4: -Potassium is necessary for the normal functioning of all cells. It regulates the heartbeat, ensures proper function of the muscles and nerves, and is vital for synthesizing protein and metabolizing carbohydrates.
