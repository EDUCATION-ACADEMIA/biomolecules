Water is extremely important to your health because ____

(A) it makes us sweat.

(B) it gives us energy.

(C) ** it is vital in all of the body’s major functions.

(D) it helps make our blood thicker so it can carry more oxygen.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Water helps your body: Keep a normal temperature. Lubricate and cushion joints. Protect your spinal cord and other sensitive tissues. Get rid of wastes through urination, perspiration, and bowel movements.

Concept note-2: -It’s essential to keeping your body functioning properly and feeling healthy. Nearly all of your body’s major systems depend on water to function and survive. With water making up about 60% of your body weight, it’s no surprise what staying hydrated can do for you.

Concept note-3: -Water’s importance in the human body can be loosely categorized into four basic functions: transportation vehicle, medium for chemical reactions, lubricant/shock absorber, and temperature regulator.
