How can a human obtain vitamins and minerals?

(A) By exercising each day

(B) ** By eating healthy foods

(C) By sleeping through the night

(D) By drinking 8 ounces of water

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Eating plenty of fruits, vegetables, wholegrains and cereals, lean meats and reduced fat dairy products will give your body the vitamins and minerals it needs, at the right level and in the right balance. There are 13 vitamins in total – 8 of these come from the B-group of vitamins.

Concept note-2: -Eating a mix of foods is the best way to get all the vitamins and minerals you need each day. Fruits and vegetables, whole grains, low-fat dairy products, lean meats, fish, and poultry are the best choices for getting the nutrients your body needs.

Concept note-3: -There are 13 essential vitamins-vitamins A, C, D, E, K, and the B vitamins (thiamine, riboflavin, niacin, pantothenic acid, biotin, B6, B12, and folate). Vitamins have different jobs to help keep the body working properly.
