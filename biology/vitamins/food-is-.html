Food is ____

(A) ** any solid or liquid that can provide the body with nutrients

(B) only solids that can provide the body with nutrients

(C) only liquids that can provide the body with nutrients

(D) any solid or liquid that can provide the body with minerals

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nutrients are chemical compounds in food that are used by the body to function properly and maintain health. Examples include proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals.

Concept note-2: -For most people, the difference between a solid and liquid is easy to discern-liquids move, they flow, they take the shape of whatever they are put in. Solids on the other hand, are rigid and donâ€™t slosh around.

Concept note-3: -Furthermore, the sensation of satiety occurs more rapidly following solid food consumption compared to liquid consumption and exists for a longer time period, delaying the return of hunger [12].

Concept note-4: -The muscles of the small intestine mix food with digestive juices from the pancreas, liver, and intestine and push the mixture forward to help with further digestion. The walls of the small intestine absorb the digested nutrients into the bloodstream. The blood delivers the nutrients to the rest of the body.
