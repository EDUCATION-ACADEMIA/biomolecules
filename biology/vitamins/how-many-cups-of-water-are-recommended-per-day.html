How many cups of water are recommended per day?

(A) 12 cups

(B) 4 cups

(C) 16 cups

(D) ** 8 cups

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Based on ALL the best evidence to date, authorities from Europe, the U.S. Institute of Medicine, and the World Health Organization recommend between 2.0 and 2.7 liters (8 to 11 cups) of water a day for women, and 2.5 to 3.7 liters (10 to 15 cups) a day for men.

Concept note-2: -1/2 gallon = 8 cups. 64 ounces = 8 cups.

Concept note-3: -Health experts commonly recommend eight 8-ounce glasses, which equals about 2 liters, or half a gallon a day. This is called the 8×8 rule and is very easy to remember.

Concept note-4: -(8 glasses measures out to 1.89 liters.)
