Fruits and vegetables can help stop:

(A) ** heart disease

(B) ** high blood pressure

(C) ** high cholesterol and some types of cancer

(D) hearing loss

(E) ** vision loss

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Carrots, sweet potatoes, squash, eggs, and green leafy vegetables. Salmon, sardines, flax seeds, soybeans, and walnuts.

Concept note-2: -Strawberries, cranberries, blackberries and blueberries are great superfoods for healthy functioning eyes. Berries are packed with nutritious vitamins and minerals and are of particular benefit in maintaining eye health.

Concept note-3: -Food for sight Many of the vitamins and antioxidants that improve eyesight naturally are found in common foods, including: Carrots, kale, spinach, and collard greens (vitamin A and lutein) Liver (vitamin A), including cod liver oil. Swiss chard, zucchini, and brussel sprouts (lutein)
