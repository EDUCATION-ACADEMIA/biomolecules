Proteins and carbohydrates are polymers.

(A) ** SURE NA SURE

(B) DI AKO SURE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Protein is a compound that is made of chains of amino acids, so it is considered to be a polymer. Carbohydrates are organic compounds of hydrogen, carbon and oxygen. They take the shape of chains of repeating units. So, they are polymers.

Concept note-2: -The best explanation for why carbohydrates and proteins are classified as polymers is because they are made up of many similar small units, monomers.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: Proteins are considered polymers because they are made of small repeating units, while lipids are not. The repeating unit of proteins are amino acids.

Concept note-4: -Biomacromolecules are large biological polymers, such as nucleic acids, proteins, and carbohydrates, that are made up of monomers linked together. For example, proteins are composed of monomers called amino acids.
