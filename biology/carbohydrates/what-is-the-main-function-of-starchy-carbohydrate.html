What is the main function of starchy carbohydrate?

(A) ** it provides us with energy

(B) It provides us with fibre

(C) It provides us with vitamin C

(D) It makes us feel full

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Starchy foods are the primary source of carbohydrates for most people. They play a crucial role in a nutritious, well-balanced diet, as they provide the body with glucose, which is the main energy source for every cell. They also provide a range of vitamins, minerals, fiber, and other nutrients.

Concept note-2: -Starchy foods are the main source of carbohydrate and play an important role in a healthy diet. They are also a good source of energy and the main source of a range of nutrients in your diet. As well as starch, they contain fibre, calcium, iron and B vitamins.

Concept note-3: -Starch has significant value for humans: it serves as the main carbohydrate source in an equilibrated diet and as a renewable raw material for industry. For instance, starch is extensively used as a thickener and texturizer in processed foods, as it gelatinizes to form pastes when heated in water.

Concept note-4: -They can also contain fiber, vitamins, minerals, naturally occurring sugars, and more. Because they are high in starch, a carbohydrate, starchy foods provide the body with readily available energy. Carbohydrates are the main source of energy in a person’s diet.

Concept note-5: -The Role of Carbohydrates: From Energy to Gut Health They’re the body’s primary source of energy and the brain’s preferred energy source. Carbs are broken down by the body into glucose – a type of sugar. Glucose is used as fuel by your body’s cells, tissues, and organs.
