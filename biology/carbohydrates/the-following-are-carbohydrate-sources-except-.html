The following are carbohydrate sources, except ____

(A) Rice, corn, and wheat

(B) ** Crackers, rice, and fruit

(C) Sweet potato, cassava, noodles

(D) Wheat, rice, potato

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Carbohydrates are found in a wide array of both healthy and unhealthy foods-bread, beans, milk, popcorn, potatoes, cookies, spaghetti, soft drinks, corn, and cherry pie.

Concept note-2: -Carbohydrates in rice Rice is also a valuable source of complex carbohydrates, which is the best source of energy. In the past, rice had been generically assessed as a high-glycemic index (GI) food. GI is a measure of the relative ability of carbohydrates in food to raise blood sugar levels after eating.

Concept note-3: -Simple carbohydrates are found naturally in foods such as fruits, milk, and milk products. They are also found in processed and refined sugars such as candy, table sugar, syrups, and soft drinks.
