BIOMOLECULES

CARBOHYDRATES

[SOURCES]
When choosing carbohydrate foods, try and choose this type of carbs whenever possible

(A) ** Complex

(B) Simple

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Complex carbohydrates are found in foods such as peas, beans, whole grains, and vegetables. Both simple and complex carbohydrates are turned to glucose (blood sugar) in the body and are used as energy. Glucose is used in the cells of the body and in the brain.

Concept note-2: -Carbohydrates are an essential energy source for the body. Complex carbohydrates provide a more lasting source of energy than simple carbohydrates because they take longer to digest.

Concept note-3: -Complex carbohydrates, found mostly in whole plant foods, maintain their natural fiber and fuel your body with the energy it needs. Examples include beans, oatmeal, 100% whole-wheat bread, quinoa, barley, potatoes, sweet potatoes, and many other plant foods.
