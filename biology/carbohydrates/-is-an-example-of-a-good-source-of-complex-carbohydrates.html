____ is an example of a good source of complex carbohydrates

(A) Can of soda

(B) Poptart

(C) ** Sweet Potato

(D) Cupcake

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Complex carbohydrates, found mostly in whole plant foods, maintain their natural fiber and fuel your body with the energy it needs. Examples include beans, oatmeal, 100% whole-wheat bread, quinoa, barley, potatoes, sweet potatoes, and many other plant foods.

Concept note-2: -Sweet potatoes fall into the healthy carb category. A medium sweet potato has about 140 calories and 5 grams of fiber. Sweet potatoes also have a low glycemic index score.

Concept note-3: -The sweet potato is classed as a complex CHO but it is higher on the GI scale. In spite of this, its high levels of fibre mean you also remain full for longer. Win-win! In addition, greater levels of vitamins and minerals are found in the sweet potato compared to its white counterpart.

Concept note-4: -The benefits of complex carbs donâ€™t stop there. Foods containing fiber and starches also often packed with vitamins, minerals and antioxidants, all nutrients that are beneficial to our body. Examples of complex carbs include: Potatoes and sweet potatoes.

Concept note-5: -2. Complex carbohydrates-These are found in almost all plant foods and usually take longer for the body to digest. They are most commonly found in whole wheat bread, whole grain pasta, brown rice and starchy vegetables like corn and peas.
