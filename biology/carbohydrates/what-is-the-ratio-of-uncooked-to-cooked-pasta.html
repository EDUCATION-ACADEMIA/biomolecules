What is the ratio of uncooked to cooked pasta?

(A) ** 1:2 cups

(B) 1:3 cups

(C) 1:1 cups

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Two ounces dry pasta is equal to 1/2 cup dry pasta, which comes out to be 1 cup of cooked pasta.

Concept note-2: -You will need roughly 1 cup (or 0.9 cups to be precise) of dried pasta to obtain 2 cups of cooked pasta. Generally, the ratio of dry pasta to cooked pasta is approximately 1:1.5 to 1:2, as the dry pasta tends to expand upon boiling/cooking, and it can easily double in weight.

Concept note-3: -100g dry pasta turns into 100g*75/31 = 242g of cooked pasta.

Concept note-4: -75g of uncooked pasta weighs approximately 170g when cooked.

Concept note-5: -Measure out 1 cup (140 g) of cooked spaghetti for a large serving. Take a standard measuring cup and fill it to the brim with cooked noodles.
