A sedentary lifestyle can cause glucose to turn into body fat. What is the best synonym for “sedentary?”

(A) Healthy

(B) Hyperactive

(C) Sleep-deprived

(D) ** Inactive

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Saturated fats raise your LDL (bad) cholesterol. High LDL cholesterol increases your risk for heart disease and stroke. Weight gain. Many high-fat foods such as pizza, baked goods, and fried foods have a lot of saturated fat.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: Carbohydrates and fats (also known as lipids) are both types of macromolecules. Chemically, the monomers of these two groups both contain the same three elements: carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen.

Concept note-3: -In fact, fat is an essential part of a balanced diet. Your body needs dietary fat for many different biological processes. Not getting enough fat can make it harder for your body to function the way it should and can lead to health issues.

Concept note-4: -Fats help the body stockpile certain nutrients as well. The so-called “fat-soluble” vitamins-A, D, E and K-are stored in the liver and in fatty tissues.
