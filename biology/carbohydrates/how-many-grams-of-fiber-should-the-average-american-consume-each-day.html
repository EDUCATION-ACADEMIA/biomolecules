How many grams of fiber should the average American consume each day?

(A) 10-20 grams

(B) ** 20-35 grams

(C) 4-9 grams

(D) 45-70 grams

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Women should try to eat at least 21 to 25 grams of fiber a day, while men should aim for 30 to 38 grams a day.

Concept note-2: -Total dietary fiber intake should be 25 to 30 grams a day from food, not supplements. Currently, dietary fiber intakes among adults in the United States average about 15 grams a day.
