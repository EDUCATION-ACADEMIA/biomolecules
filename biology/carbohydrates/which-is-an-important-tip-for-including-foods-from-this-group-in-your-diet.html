Which is an important tip for including foods from this group in your diet?

(A) ** Eat a wide variety of foods from this group

(B) Just eat the foods that you like

(C) Eat the same foods at the same time everyday

(D) Only eat these foods early in the day, not after lunchtime

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Eating a varied, well-balanced diet means eating a variety of foods from each of the 5 food groups daily, in the recommended amounts. Because different foods provide different types and amounts of key nutrients, it is important to choose a variety of foods from within each food group.

Concept note-2: -According to the U.S. Department of Agriculture, you should consume the highest proportion of your foods from the grain group. However, you should consume almost as many portions from the vegetable group; fill your plate a little over one-fourth full with grains and add almost the same amount of vegetables.

Concept note-3: -A balanced diet is made up of foods from the five food groups: starchy carbohydrates, fruits and vegetables, protein, dairy and healthy fats. Each provides the range of vitamins and minerals our bodies need to function efficiently.
