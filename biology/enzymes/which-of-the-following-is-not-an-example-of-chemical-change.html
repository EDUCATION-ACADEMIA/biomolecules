Which of the following is NOT an example of chemical change?

(A) sour milk

(B) burning wood

(C) ** breaking a pencil

(D) rust

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Since the size of a pencil reduces once broken, it can be said as an example of physical change.

Concept note-2: -Breaking of pencil is a physical change.

Concept note-3: -Melting of ice is not an example of chemical change as the chemical composition of water remains the same even after melting.

Concept note-4: -Breaking is a physical change because the shards are still glass, but it canâ€™t be easily made whole again.

Concept note-5: -Note: Breaking a brick, evaporation of liquid, and moving a wheel, are examples of physical changes as no change is observed in the chemical composition in carrying out these processes.
