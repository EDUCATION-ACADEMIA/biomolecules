In the formula:C + O2 CO2, CO2 is the ____

(A) ** Products

(B) Solution

(C) Answer

(D) Reactants

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is a combination reaction as carbon combines with oxygen to form carbon dioxide.

Concept note-2: -There is only one product, CO2. The reactants, C and O2, combine to form CO2.

Concept note-3: -Therefore, the chemical reaction where carbon dioxide is a product is answer choice (A): C plus O2 react to produce CO2.

Concept note-4: -Combustion: Combustion reactions involve the reaction of a substance with oxygen gas (O2).

Concept note-5: -At ordinary temperatures, carbon dioxide is quite unreactive; above 1, 700 °C (3, 100 °F) it partially decomposes into carbon monoxide and oxygen. Hydrogen or carbon also convert it to carbon monoxide at high temperatures.
