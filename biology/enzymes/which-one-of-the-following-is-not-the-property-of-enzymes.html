ENZYMES

[SOURCES]
which one of the following is NOT the property of enzymes?

(A) enzyme actions are specific

(B) enzymes can be reused

(C) enzymes are affected by pH

(D) ** Optimum temperature of all enzymes is 37

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The optimum temperature for most enzymes is about 98.6 degrees Fahrenheit (37 degrees Celsius). There are also enzymes that work well at lower and higher temperatures. For example, Arctic animals have enzymes adapted to lower optimal temperatures; animals in desert climates have enzymes adapted to higher temperatures.

Concept note-2: -This optimal temperature is usually around human body temperature (37.5 oC) for the enzymes in human cells. Above this temperature the enzyme structure begins to break down (denature) since at higher temperatures intra-and intermolecular bonds are broken as the enzyme molecules gain even more kinetic energy.

Concept note-3: -Final answer: Enzymes are not used in chemical reactions is not true for enzymes.

Concept note-4: -Optimum temperature is the temperature at which enzyme activity is maximum. In humans, this temperature is 37.5oC that is same as the normal body temperature. A rise in temperature above 40oC results in denaturation of the proteins of enzyme. Thus the correct answer is option A.
