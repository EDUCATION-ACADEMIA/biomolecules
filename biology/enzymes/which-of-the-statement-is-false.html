Which of the statement is FALSE?

(A) Extreme changes in acidity or alkalinity of the solution denatures the enzymes

(B) ** Denaturation can be reversible under optimum temperature

(C) Denaturing can occur because of heat, acid or base.

(D) Denaturation means that the rate of reaction is reduced

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Protein denaturation is said to be irreversible when the denatured state achieved by increasing temperature or by using chemical denaturants is unable to return to the native, biologically functional state upon removal of the factor that caused denaturation.

Concept note-2: -Final answer: The wrong statement about the denaturation of proteins is fibrous proteins are converted into globular proteins.

Concept note-3: -In many cases, denaturation is reversible. Since the primary structure of protein is intact, once the denaturing influence is removed, proteins can regain their native state by folding back to the original conformation. This process is called renaturation.

Concept note-4: -In many cases, denaturation is reversible (the proteins can regain their native state when the denaturing influence is removed). This process can be called renaturation.
