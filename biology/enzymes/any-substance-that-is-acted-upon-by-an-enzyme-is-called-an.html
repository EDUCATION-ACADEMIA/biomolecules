Any substance that is acted upon by an enzyme is called a(n)?

(A) Coenzyme

(B) ** Substrate

(C) Vitamin

(D) Polypeptide

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -substrate: A reactant in a chemical reaction is called a substrate when acted upon by an enzyme.

Concept note-2: -The substance which reacts with the enzyme to form the product is called substrate. The part of the enzyme where the substrate binds is called the active site. The enzyme reacts with the substrate to form enzyme-substrate complex followed by formation of the product.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: The substance on which an enzyme acts upon is called the substrate.
