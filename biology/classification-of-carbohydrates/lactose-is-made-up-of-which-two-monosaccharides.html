Lactose is made up of which two monosaccharides?

(A) Glucose and fructose

(B) ** Glucose and galactose

(C) glucose and glucose

(D) glucose and maltose

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lactose is a disaccharide consisting of the monomers glucose and galactose. It is found naturally in milk.

Concept note-2: -Lactose is the disaccharides which are present in milk. it is also called milk sugar. It is made up of two monosaccharides glucose and galactose. Lactose is formed by beta 1-4 condensation of two hexose molecules.

Concept note-3: -Common Disaccharides Lactose is a disaccharide consisting of the monomers glucose and galactose.

Concept note-4: -Lactose is a disaccharide consisting of glucose and galactose and is found naturally in milk.
