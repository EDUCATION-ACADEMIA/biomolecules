The type of polysaccharide that is used as an energy source in cells.

(A) Heparin

(B) Structural Polysaccharide

(C) Acidic Polysaccharide

(D) ** Storage Polysaccharide

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Glycogen: It is made up of a large chain of molecules.

Concept note-2: -Glycogen is a polysaccharide used as energy storage in animals. Glycogen is a polymer made up of glucose units and undergoes hydrolysis to release glucose when demand for sugar increases.

Concept note-3: -a polysaccharide that occurs in a living organism as a form of stored energy: storage polysaccharides include the glycogen in animals and the starch and fructosan in plants.
