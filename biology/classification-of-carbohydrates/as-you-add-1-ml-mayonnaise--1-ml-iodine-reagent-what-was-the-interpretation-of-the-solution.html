[SOURCES]
As you add 1 mL Mayonnaise + 1 mL Iodine reagent, what was the interpretation of the solution?

(A) absence of reducing sugar

(B) presence of reducing sugar

(C) presence of starch

(D) ** absence of starch

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The result is positive. According to the observation the food sample or the potato slice turned to blue-black on adding the iodine solution. This proves the presence of starch in the given plant source.

Concept note-2: -On adding the iodine solution to starch, the two react to produce a blue-black color. Q. Rice is a rich source of starch and iodine gives blue black colour with starch.

Concept note-3: -Using an iodine solution, you can test for the presence of starch. When starch is present, the iodine changes from brown to blue-black or purple.

Concept note-4: -A positive result for the iodine test (starch is present) was a colour change ranging from violet to black; a negative result (no starch) was the yellow colour of the iodine solution.
