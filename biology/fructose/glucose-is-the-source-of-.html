[SOURCES]
Glucose is the source of ____

(A) some health problems

(B) energy production

(C) sugar within some foods

(D) ** all of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In energy metabolism, glucose is the most important source of energy in all organisms. Glucose for metabolism is stored as a polymer, in plants mainly as starch and amylopectin, and in animals as glycogen. Glucose circulates in the blood of animals as blood sugar.

Concept note-2: -Glucose is the main type of sugar in the blood and is the major source of energy for the bodyâ€™s cells. Glucose comes from the foods we eat or the body can make it from other substances. Glucose is carried to the cells through the bloodstream.

Concept note-3: -The sources are: 1. From Carbohydrates of the Diet 2. From Various Glucogenic Compounds 3. From Liver Glycogen by Glycogenolysis.

Concept note-4: -We have two sources of glucose: 1) food, 2) products of metabolism. Food contains carbohydrates, lipids, proteins, etc. Dietary carbohydrates are digested to yield simple sugar molecules in the gut.
