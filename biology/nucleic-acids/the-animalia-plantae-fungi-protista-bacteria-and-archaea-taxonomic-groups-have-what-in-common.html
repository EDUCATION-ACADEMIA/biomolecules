The animalia, plantae, fungi, protista, bacteria, and archaea taxonomic groups have what in common?

(A) Oxygen is essential for metabolism

(B) Nutrition is by absorption or ingestion

(C) All organisms have the same genetic code

(D) ** DNA is present in cells

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -They are all made up of multi-celled organisms with membrane bound organelles.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: Protista, Fungi, Plantae, and Animalia are all eukaryotes.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: Protoctista, Animalia, Plantae, and Fungi kingdoms are all in domain Eukarya. This means they are all eukaryotes, made of eukaryotic cells.

Concept note-4: -Eukaryotic cells have a true nucleus, which means the cell’s DNA is surrounded by a membrane. Therefore, the nucleus houses the cell’s DNA and directs the synthesis of proteins and ribosomes, the cellular organelles responsible for protein synthesis.
