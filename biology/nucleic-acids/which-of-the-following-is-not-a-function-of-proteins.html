Which of the following is NOT a function of PROTEINS?

(A) structural building block

(B) enzymatic role

(C) cell transport

(D) ** stores genetic information

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -However, proteins do not provide energy for metabolism.

Concept note-2: -Proteins help fight disease, control the rate of reaction and move substances into or out of cells. However, proteins do not store and transmit genetic information and thus the correct answer is A.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: The correct answer is (E) Transmit hereditary information. Proteins have many different roles in living organisms.

Concept note-4: -They also assist with the formation of new molecules by reading the genetic information stored in DNA. Messenger proteins, such as some types of hormones, transmit signals to coordinate biological processes between different cells, tissues, and organs. These proteins provide structure and support for cells.

Concept note-5: -No, proteins do not store genetic information or transmit to the next generation. The genetic information is stored in the form of nucleic acids i.e. deoxyribonucleic acids (DNA) or ribonucleic acids (RNA).
