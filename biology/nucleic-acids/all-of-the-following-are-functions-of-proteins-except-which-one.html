All of the following are functions of proteins except which one?

(A) Transport oxygen

(B) Speed up chemical reactions

(C) Provide immunity

(D) ** Short term energy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Protein is considered a long-term energy source when compare to carbohydrates as energy sources. The reason for this is that proteins take longer to metabolize than carbohydrates. The average metabolism rate for protein is 12 hours compared to carbohydrates which is between 15 minutes to 5 hours at the most.

Concept note-2: -The correct option is D carrying genetic information.

Concept note-4: -The major functions of proteins are providing structure, regulating body processes, transporting materials, balancing fluids, helping with immunity, and providing energy.
