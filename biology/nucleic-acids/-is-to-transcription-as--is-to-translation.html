____ is to transcription as ____ is to translation.

(A) ** mRNA / Amino Acid Chain

(B) Amino Acid Chain / mRNA

(C) Ribosome / Nucleus

(D) mRNA / tRNA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Messenger RNA (mRNA) is a kind of RNA that conveys genetic information from DNA to protein by way of the processes of transcription and translation.

Concept note-2: -Translation, as related to genomics, is the process through which information encoded in messenger RNA (mRNA) directs the addition of amino acids during protein synthesis.
