There are three different types of RNA. They are ____ (select all!)

(A) ** Messenger

(B) Transport

(C) ** Transfer

(D) ** Ribosomal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Three main types of RNA are involved in protein synthesis. They are messenger RNA (mRNA), transfer RNA (tRNA), and ribosomal RNA (rRNA).

Concept note-2: -Types of rRNA Therefore, it may be concluded that a single rRNA gene in archaea and bacteria codes for the three rRNA types: 16S, 23S, and 5S.

Concept note-3: -Prokaryotes have 3 varieties of rRNA: 5S, 16S and 23S rRNAs. The 5S and 23S rRNAs are found within the large ribosomal subunit, while the 16S rRNA is located in the small ribosomal subunit. Eukaryotes, on the other hand, have at least 4 flavors of rRNA: 5S, 5.8S, 18S and 28S rRNAs.
