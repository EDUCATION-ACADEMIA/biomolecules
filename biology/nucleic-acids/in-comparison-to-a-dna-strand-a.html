In comparison to a DNA strand, a

(A) ** mRNA strand is shorter and single stranded

(B) mRNA strand is longer and single stranded

(C) mRNA strand is shorter and double stranded

(D) mRNA strand is longer and double stranded

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DNA is a double-stranded molecule that has a long chain of nucleotides. RNA is a single-stranded molecule which has a shorter chain of nucleotides. DNA replicates on its own, it is self-replicating. RNA does not replicate on its own.

Concept note-2: -In addition, because they are copied from only a limited region of the DNA, RNA molecules are much shorter than DNA molecules. A DNA molecule in a human chromosome can be up to 250 million nucleotide-pairs long; in contrast, most RNAs are no more than a few thousand nucleotides long, and many are considerably shorter.

Concept note-3: -Physically, mRNA is a strand of nucleotides known as ribonucleic acid, and is single-stranded.

Concept note-4: -An mRNA transcript is a single strand of RNA that encapsulate the information contained in a gene. Think of an mRNA transcript as a portable gene: smaller and more mobile than the DNA sequence that it is built from, but containing the same information.

Concept note-5: -Messenger RNA (abbreviated mRNA) is a type of single-stranded RNA involved in protein synthesis. mRNA is made from a DNA template during the process of transcription.
