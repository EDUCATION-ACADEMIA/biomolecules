Which of the following do fats do?

(A) ** Store energy

(B) ** Insulate body

(C) ** Cushion organs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fat cushions vital organs, insulates our bodies from the cold, and serves as our fuel storage tank, keeping our demanding metabolic system operating.

Concept note-2: -White fat cells (adipocytes) have a simple structure composed of a single lipid droplet (fat molecule) and a few cellular organelles. They provide energy storage, insulation from extreme temperatures and cushioning around soft organs.

Concept note-3: -Adipose tissue helps to store energy in the form of fat, cushion internal organs, and insulate the body.

Concept note-4: -Fats function as a cushion and shock absorber for eyeballs, kidneys, and gonads. Vitamins A, D, E, K require fat for their absorption and transport.
