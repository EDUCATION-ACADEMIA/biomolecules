Randy is in a weightlifting class. Which food would provide the proteins he needs for building muscle mass?

(A) Strawberry

(B) Crackers

(C) ** Beans

(D) Hershey Bar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -High protein foods such as chicken, salmon, Greek yogurt, skim milk, and beans are some of the best foods to help you gain lean muscle (67).

Concept note-2: -Whey is considered a “fast-acting” protein, while casein takes longer for the body to process – and is the reason why many athletes turn to dairy proteins since they provide a sustained release of amino acids over a longer period of time. However, animal proteins aren’t necessary in order to build muscle.

Concept note-3: -BLACK BEANS 22g Protein (44% DV) A great non-meat alternative that’s high in protein and great for building muscle is black beans. With an impressive 22g of protein packed into just 100g serving, use them as a substitute for rice and pasta when trying to build muscle.
