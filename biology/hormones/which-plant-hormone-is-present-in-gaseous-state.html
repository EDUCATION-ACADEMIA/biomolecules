Which plant hormone is present in gaseous state?

(A) auxin

(B) gibberellin

(C) ** ethylene

(D) cytokinin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Among them only ethylene is found in gaseous form. Hence, the option (c) Ethylene is the correct answer. Additional Information: Ethylene hormone can be produced from almost any part of the plant.

Concept note-2: -Ethylene, often known as the ripening hormone, is a gaseous plant hormone. Ripening fruits, flowers, leaves, and even roots synthesize it. It causes changes in texture, softness, color, and other processes in fruits by triggering a series of events.

Concept note-3: -Ethylene, the Gaseous Plant Hormone.

Concept note-4: -Ethylene gas is a plant hormone that regulates plant growth, development and response to environmental stress. It is produced from leaves, roots, stems, flowers and fruits, and it plays a major role in fruit ripening, flowering and abscission (the natural shedding of leaves, flowers and fruits).

Concept note-5: -Ethylene is regarded as a multifunctional phytohormone that regulates both growth, and senescence. It promotes or inhibits growth and senescence processes depending on its concentration, timing of application, and the plant species.
