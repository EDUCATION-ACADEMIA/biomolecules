Which of the following statement is correct?

(A) The higher the auxin in the root, the higher the growth rate.

(B) The lower the auxin in the root, the lower the growth rate.

(C) ** The higher the auxin in the shoot, the higher the growth rate.

(D) The lower the auxin in the shoot, the higher the growth rate.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The application of very high concentrations of auxin inhibits the growth of shoots directly.

Concept note-2: -Correct option: a IAA and IBA are natural but NAA 2 4-D and 2 4 5-T are synthetic auxins. Explanation:Natural auxin are naturally occurring auxin plants hormone which is called photo hormones. Eg. IAA and IBB.

Concept note-3: -Auxin hormone increases the rate of growth in a stem but it decreases the rate of growth in a root.

Concept note-4: -Shoots and roots respond differently to high concentrations of auxins: cells in shoots grow more. cells in roots grow less.
