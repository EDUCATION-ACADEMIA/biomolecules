[SOURCES]
This plant hormone is responsible for delaying leaf old age.

(A) Abscisic Acid(ABA)

(B) Gibberellins

(C) ** Cytokinins

(D) Auxins

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gibberelline Is a plant hormone known to delay senescence, induce parthenocarpy and break dormancy during germination.

Concept note-2: -This suggests that cytokinin may delay leaf senescence not only as a result of exogenous treatment, but as part of an endogenous developmental program.

Concept note-3: -Cytokinin production slows down the process of senescence. However, accumulation of sugars during senescence, due to the breakdown of accumulated starch or the preferential export of N2 from the leaf, can block the effect of cytokinin, especially in low light.

Concept note-4: -Cytokinin is a plant hormone that delays leaf ageing (senescence). It promotes cell division in the presence of auxin (another type of plant growth hormone).

Concept note-5: -So, the correct answer is ‘Cytokinins’.
