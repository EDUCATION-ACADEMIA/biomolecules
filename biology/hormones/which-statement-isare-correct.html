which statement is/are correct?

(A) ** Acetylcholine act by cAMP pathway

(B) ** Gastrin act by cAMP pathway

(C) ** Hitamine act by PIP2 pathway

(D) Histamine act by cAMP pathway

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct option is b. Histamine is present in almost all tissues of the human body. It is mainly stored in the granules of mast cells.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: Of the given statements about histamines the one that is not correct is that it is a bronchodilator.

Concept note-3: -Histamine has diverse effects, both pro-inflammatory and anti-inflammatory, which are determined by both the histamine receptor subtype and the cells stimulated types (38).

Concept note-4: -We conclude that the canonical beta-catenin pathway acts downstream of the histamine receptor H1 in a variety of cell types. The observation that inflammatory molecules, like histamine, activate the beta-catenin pathway may provide a molecular explanation for a possible link between inflammation and cancer.
