Which of the following is the hormone that promotes cell division, suppresses leaf aging, and makes blooms last longer?

(A) Ethylene

(B) Gibberellin

(C) Auxin

(D) Abscisic acid

(E) ** Cytokinins

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cytokinin is a plant growth hormone that is present in in plant roots and shoots. It promotes cell division, cell growth & differentiation and faciltates the growth of a plant while working along with auxin.

Concept note-2: -Gibberellins. Gibberellins stimulate cell division and elongation, break seed dormancy, and speed germination.

Concept note-3: -Primarily, aging and the dropping of leaves are controlled by the plant growth hormone named abscisic acid (ABA). It also helps to control the dormancy of the seeds and to sustain the plant in adverse conditions.

Concept note-4: -Ethylene is regarded as a multifunctional phytohormone that regulates both growth, and senescence. It promotes or inhibits growth and senescence processes depending on its concentration, timing of application, and the plant species.

Concept note-5: -Cytokinins are plant hormones which promotes cytokinesis or cell division. It also helps in opening of stomata, delaying ageing in leaves, etc.
