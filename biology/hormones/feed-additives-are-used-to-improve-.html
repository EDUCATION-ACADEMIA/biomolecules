Concept note-1: -Feed additives are products used in animal nutrition for purposes of improving the quality of feed and the quality of food from animal origin, or to improve the animalsâ€™ performance and health, e.g. providing enhanced digestibility of the feed materials.

Concept note-2: -Stabilize rumen environment and pH. Improve growth (gain and/or feed efficiency) Reduce heat stress effects. Improve health (such as less ketosis, reduce acidosis, or improve immune response)

Concept note-3: -Natural additives in animal feed are able to improve productivity and performance by enhancing digestibility, maintaining and stabilizing beneficial microflora in the gut and finally can improve quality of animal products and positively influence the environment.

Concept note-4: -For instance, feed additives are used to enhance flavour of feed, to meet the need for certain nutrients or to increase the performance of animals in good health. They are used in feed for food-producing animals and in pet food.
