Which of the following glands does NOT belong to the endocrine system?

(A) Pituitary

(B) Thyroid

(C) Testes

(D) ** Salivary

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct option is 2 i.e Salivary gland. The Endocrine system consists of Endocrine glands that release hormones into the bloodstream.

Concept note-2: -Endocrine glands and exocrine glands are two types of glands in the human body based on presence or absence of ducts. Exocrine glands include sweat, salivary, mammary, ceruminous, lacrimal, sebaceous, and mucous glands.

Concept note-3: -There is another type of gland called an exocrine gland (e.g. sweat glands, lymph nodes). These are not considered part of the endocrine system as they do not produce hormones and they release their product through a duct.

Concept note-4: -Lacrimal gland is present in the eye and secretes tears. It is not a salivary gland.

Concept note-5: -Examples of exocrine glands include sweat glands, lacrimal glands, salivary glands, mammary glands, and digestive glands in the stomach, pancreas, and intestines.
