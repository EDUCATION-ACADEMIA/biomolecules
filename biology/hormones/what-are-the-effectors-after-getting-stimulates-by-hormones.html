What are the effectors after getting stimulates by hormones?

(A) Muscle in the GIt walls

(B) secretion of the exocrine glands

(C) ** Both of the above

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Luteinising hormone and follicle stimulating hormone, also known as gonadotrophins. They act on the ovaries or testes to stimulate sex hormone production, and egg and sperm maturity.

Concept note-2: -An effector hormone is a hormone that acts on a particular tissue-an example of such a hormone is thyroxine (T4), which regulates metabolism in many tissues throughout the body.

Concept note-3: -There are three mechanisms by which endocrine glands are stimulated to synthesize and release hormones: humoral stimuli, hormonal stimuli, and neural stimuli.
