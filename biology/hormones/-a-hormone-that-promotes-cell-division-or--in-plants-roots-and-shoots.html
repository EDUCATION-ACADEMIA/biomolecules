____ A hormone that promotes cell division, or ____, in plants roots and shoots

(A) ** Cytokinins

(B) Abscisic acid

(C) ** Cytokinesis

(D) Auxins

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cytokinins are plant hormones which promotes cytokinesis or cell division. It also helps in opening of stomata, delaying ageing in leaves, etc.

Concept note-2: -The stimulating growth factor was found to be cytokinin, a hormone that promotes cytokinesis (cell division). Almost 200 naturally-occurring or synthetic cytokinins are known, to date.

Concept note-3: -Auxin (indole-3-acetic acid, IAA), produced in young shoot organs, promotes root development and induces vascular differentiation.

Concept note-4: -Cytokinin is the hormone that is vital for cell proliferation and differentiation.
