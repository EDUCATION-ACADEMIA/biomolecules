Which is not an example of a flowering plant?

(A) roses

(B) oak trees

(C) ** mosses

(D) tomato plants

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Non-flowering plants include mosses, liverworts, hornworts, lycophytes and ferns and reproduce by spores. Some non-flowering plants, called gymnosperms or conifers, still produce seeds.

Concept note-2: -Mosses are non-flowering plants which produce spores and have stems and leaves, but donâ€™t have true roots. Mosses, and their cousins liverworts and hornworts, are classified as Bryophyta (bryophytes) in the plant kingdom.

Concept note-3: -Non-flowering plants include ferns, clubmosses, horsetails, mosses, lichens, and fungi. These are spore-producing plants, a major feature distinguishing them from the seed-producing flowering plants.

Concept note-4: -Algae, Fungi, Mosses, and ferns, etc are all non-flowering plants. Plants that do not produce flowers and seeds are called non-flowering plants. Non-flowering plants are called as Cryptogamous plants. Plants such as ferns reproduce using spores instead of seeds.
