CLASSIFICATION OF VITAMINS

[SOURCES]
Cherries, apricots, peaches and plums are all fruits in which category?

(A) berries

(B) melons

(C) ** drupes

(D) tropical fruits

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So many delicious fruits like peaches, plums, apricots, dates, mangoes, coconuts, and cherries fall into the stone fruit category. Even olives, though we often think of them more as savory, are stone fruits!

Concept note-2: -Prunus is a genus of trees and shrubs, which includes (among many others) the fruits plums, cherries, peaches, nectarines, apricots, and almonds. L.

Concept note-3: -Typical drupes include apricots, olives, loquat, peaches, plums, cherries, mangoes, pecans, and amlas (Indian gooseberries). Other examples include sloe (Prunus spinosa) and ivy (Hedera helix).

Concept note-4: -Stone fruits are deciduous tree species originating from the temperate zone of the northern hemisphere. Plum, peach and nectarine, sweet and sour (tart) cherry, apricot, and cornelian cherry belong to the group of stone fruits according to pomological classification.
