Which category of fruit is characterized by a thick inedible rind, juicy flesh, and many seeds in the center?

(A) ** melons

(B) pomes

(C) drupes

(D) citrus fruits

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Citrus fruits (oranges, tangerines, tangelos, grapefruits) have thick rinds with thin membranes inside that separate the flesh into segments.

Concept note-2: -According to Fruit from Spain, fruit is often categorized into six main groups: berries, pits, cores, citrus fruits, melons and tropical fruit.

Concept note-3: -Any fruit or 100% fruit juice counts as part of the Fruit Group. Fruits may be fresh, canned, frozen, or dried, and may be whole, cut-up, or pureed. Visit the Fruits Group page for more information on fruits.
