Which two organisms are least closely related?

(A) ** two organisms from the same KINGDOM

(B) two organisms from the same ORDER

(C) two organisms from the same FAMILY

(D) two organisms from the same GENUS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Group organisms are least closely related.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: Organisms belonging to the same B) family would be the most closely related.

Concept note-3: -Amoeba and Euglena are more closely related to each other than any other pair of organisms as they both belong to the Phylum Protista and show similar characteristics.

Concept note-4: -The organism that is least closely related to land plants is Euglena. Evidence that supports this answer includes the fact that Euglena is a protist, while land plants are multicellular organisms classified as plants.
