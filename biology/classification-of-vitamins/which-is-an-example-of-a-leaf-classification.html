Which is an example of a leaf classification?

(A) ** Kale

(B) Asparagus

(C) Pea

(D) Cauliflower

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kale is a popular vegetable and a member of the cabbage family. It is a cruciferous vegetable and is closely related to cabbage, broccoli, cauliflower, collard greens, and Brussels sprouts. There are many different types of kale. The leaves can be green or purple, and have either a smooth or curly shape.

Concept note-2: -What Is Kale? Kale is a dark, leafy green you can eat raw or cooked. This superfood has been on dinner plates since Roman times and has long been common across much of Europe. The vegetable hails from the cabbage family, which also includes broccoli, cauliflower, and collards.
