CLASSIFICATION OF VITAMINS

[SOURCES]
Carbohydrates, Proteins, and Fats are included in the classification of ____

(A) Undesirable fats

(B) Simple carbs

(C) ** Macronutrients

(D) Micronutrients

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Carbohydrates, fat and protein are called macronutrients. They are the nutrients you use in the largest amounts. “Macronutrients are the nutritive components of food that the body needs for energy and to maintain the body’s structure and systems, ” says MD Anderson Wellness Dietitian Lindsey Wohlford.

Concept note-2: -There are three types of macronutrients: carbohydrates, proteins, and fats. Along with energy, all of these macronutrients have specific roles in your body that allows you to function properly.

Concept note-3: -Carbohydrates, fat, and protein are macronutrients. Micronutrients, however, are needed in smaller amounts, and these include vitamins and minerals. Macronutrients provide your body with energy.

Concept note-4: -Macronutrients are types of foods that are needed in large quantities in the diet. These include carbohydrates, proteins, fats, cholesterol, fiber, and water.
