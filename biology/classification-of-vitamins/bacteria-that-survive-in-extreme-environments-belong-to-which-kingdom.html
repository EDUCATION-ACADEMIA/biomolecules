Bacteria that survive in extreme environments belong to which kingdom?

(A) Kingdom Fungi

(B) Kingdom Protista

(C) Kingdom Eubacteria

(D) ** Kingdom Archaebacteria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Most extremophiles are microorganisms (and a high proportion of these are archaea), but this group also includes eukaryotes such as protists (e.g., algae, fungi and protozoa) and multicellular organisms. Archaea is the main group to thrive in extreme environments.

Concept note-2: -Archaebacteria are known to be the oldest living organisms on earth. They belong to the kingdom Monera and are classified as bacteria because they resemble bacteria when observed under a microscope.

Concept note-3: -Archaea are famous for their love of living in extreme environments. If it’s super hot (more than 100° Celsius), freezing, acidic, alkaline, salty, deep in the ocean, even bombarded by gamma or UV radiation, there’s probably life there, and that life is probably archaeal species.
