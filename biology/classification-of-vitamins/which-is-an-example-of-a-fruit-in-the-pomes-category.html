Which is an example of a fruit in the pomes category?

(A) Watermelon

(B) Raspberry

(C) Lemon

(D) ** Apple

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apples, pears, nashi and quince are pome fruits, grown from spring blossom and harvested from late summer through to late autumn.

Concept note-2: -What do apples, quince and pears all have in common? They’re all pome fruits! The word pome is derived from Latin translation of the word “fruit”. This fruit family is borne from flowering plants in the Rosaceae, or rose, family.

Concept note-3: -Apples, pears, and quince are pome fruits. The word pome is from the Latin word pomum, meaning apple. Pome fruits have leathery seeds surrounded by a core.

Concept note-4: -The best-known example of a pome is the apple. Other examples of plants that produce fruit classified as a pome are Cotoneaster, Crataegus (hawthorn and mayhaw), medlar, pear, Pyracantha, quince, rowan, loquat, toyon, and whitebeam.
