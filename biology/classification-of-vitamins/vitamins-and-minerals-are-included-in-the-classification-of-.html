Vitamins and Minerals are included in the classification of ____

(A) Microminerals

(B) Macronutrients

(C) ** Micronutrients

(D) Macrominerals

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vitamins and minerals are micronutrients required by the body to carry out a range of normal functions. However, these micronutrients are not produced in our bodies and must be derived from the food we eat.

Concept note-2: -Micronutrients can be divided into four groups-water-soluble vitamins, fat-soluble vitamins, macrominerals and trace minerals.

Concept note-3: -Micronutrients are nutrients required by the body in smaller amounts, but theyâ€™re still essential for carrying out bodily functions. Micronutrients include all of the essential minerals and vitamins.

Concept note-4: -Vitamins and minerals are often called micronutrients because your body needs only tiny amounts of them.
