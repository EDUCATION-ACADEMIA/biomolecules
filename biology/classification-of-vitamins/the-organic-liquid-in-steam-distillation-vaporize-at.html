The organic liquid in steam distillation vaporize at

(A) ** Lower temperature than its boiling point

(B) Higher temperature than its boiling point

(C) At its boiling point

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution : In steam distillation, the mixture boils when the sum of vapour pressure of water and organic liquid becomes equal to the atmospheric pressure. Since the vapour pressure of water is appreciably higher than that of the organic liquid, it vaporises at a temperature much lower than its normal boiling point.

Concept note-2: -An organic liquid vaporizes at a temperature its boiling point in steam distillation.
