Which of the following best describes a stop codon?

(A) ** A stop codon signals that the protein is finished being built

(B) A stop codon signals that the ribosome needs to pause before continuing to build the protein

(C) A stop codon signals that the amino acid chain should break in half midway through construction

(D) A stop codon signals that the tRNA’s should only retrieve 3 more amino acids to finish building the protein

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1) Stop Codon: Stop codons is a mRNA which is a sequence of three nucleotides. It signals to stop the protein synthesis in the cell.

Concept note-2: -There are 3 STOP codons in the genetic code-UAG, UAA, and UGA. These codons signal the end of the polypeptide chain during translation.

Concept note-3: -A stop codon is a sequence of three nucleotides (a trinucleotide) in DNA or messenger RNA (mRNA) that signals a halt to protein synthesis in the cell.

Concept note-4: -Thus the correct answer is (B). UGA, UAG, UAA are called stop codons.

Concept note-5: -UGA, UAA, and UAG are stop codons.
