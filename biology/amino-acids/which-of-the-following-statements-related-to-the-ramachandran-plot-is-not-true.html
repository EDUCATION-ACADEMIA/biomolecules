Which of the following statements related to the Ramachandran plot is not true?

(A) ** Almost all peptide bonds in proteins are cis.

(B) The restriction of psi and phi angles limits structures accessible to unfolded form of proteins.

(C) A configuration that has ϕ =90o and ψ =-900 is disallowed.

(D) Three quarters of possible phi and psi values are excluded by their steric clashes.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Peptides that are unstructured will have all the backbone dihedral angles in the. disallowed regions.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: Glycine and proline are an exception to the Ramachandran plot.

Concept note-3: -The Ramachandran plot shows the statistical distribution of the combinations of the backbone dihedral angles and . In theory, the allowed regions of the Ramachandran plot show which values of the Phi/Psi angles are possible for an amino acid, X, in a ala-X-ala tripeptide (Ramachandran et al., 1963).

Concept note-4: -Updated On: 27-06-2022 Due to resonance, C-N bond length in proteins is smaller than usual bond length of C−N bond. Therefore, option (a) is incorrect.
