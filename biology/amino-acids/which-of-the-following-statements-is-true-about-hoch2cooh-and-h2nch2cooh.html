AMINO ACIDS

[SOURCES]
Which of the following statements is TRUE about HOCH2COOH and H2NCH2COOH?

(A) Both are insoluble in water.

(B) Both can form zwitterion.

(C) Both can be obtained from hydrolysis of protein.

(D) ** Both can easily dissolve in sodium hydroxide solution.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution : As ‘Zn(OH) (2), BeO, Al (2)O (3) ‘ are amphoteric, so they can react with both HCl and NaOH.

Concept note-2: -So, hydrochloric acid reacts with sodium hydroxide solution to form sodium chloride and water. This reaction can be represented as: Hydrochloric acid + Sodium hydroxide → Sodium chloride + Water. Q.

Concept note-3: -"When sodium peroxide dissolves in water, it is hydrolyzed and forms sodium hydroxide and hydrogen peroxide."

Concept note-4: -When sodium hydroxide and hydrochloric acid react, they neutralise each other and form common salt (salt) and water. So, the given statement is true.
