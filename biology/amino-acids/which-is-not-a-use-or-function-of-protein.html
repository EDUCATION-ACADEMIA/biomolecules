Which is not a use or function of protein?

(A) forms enzymes and hormones

(B) forms transport channel in cell membrane

(C) forms structural proteins in the body

(D) ** quick energy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -However, proteins do not provide energy for metabolism.

Concept note-2: -Provides Energy. Proteins can supply your body with energy. Protein contains four calories per gram, the same amount of energy that carbs provide. Fats supply the most energy, at nine calories per gram.

Concept note-3: -Final answer: DNA is not a protein.

Concept note-4: -Under ordinary circumstances, our bodies only use protein for a small amount of daily energy. Protein is meant for growth and repair and cannot be stored away like fat and glycogen.

Concept note-5: -All of these are functions of proteins except (d) storing genetic information of living things, which is the role of deoxyribonucleic acid (DNA). Structural proteins (a) provide structural components that provide strength and protection for biological structures (e.g. keratin in hair).
