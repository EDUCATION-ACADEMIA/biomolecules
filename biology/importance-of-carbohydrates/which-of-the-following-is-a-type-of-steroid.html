Concept note-3: -4 Types of Injectable Testosterone.

Concept note-4: -The main types are: tablets, syrups and liquids – such as prednisolone.

Concept note-5: -Is testosterone a steroid? Natural testosterone is a steroid-an anabolic-androgenic steroid. “Anabolic” refers to muscle building, and “androgenic” refers to increased male sex characteristics.
