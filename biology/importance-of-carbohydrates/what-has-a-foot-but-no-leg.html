What has a foot but no leg?

(A) A sandwich

(B) A stool

(C) A keyboard

(D) ** A ruler

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1. What has a foot but no leg? The answer is Ruler.

Concept note-2: -Answer: A snail is an animal that has feet but no legs. The answer to the abovementioned riddle, “I have feet, but no legs.

Concept note-3: -A Yardstick has three feet used for measuring but no toes.

Concept note-4: -What has one foot but no body? Answer: A ruler.

Concept note-5: -There are animals that have two feet, but they can’t actually walk on them. This group of animals includes the giraffe, hippopotamus, and whales. These creatures use their legs for balance and to move around, but they don’t have fully developed walking muscles.
